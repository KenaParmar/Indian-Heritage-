Ugadi

Ugadi, also known as Yugadi, is a major festival celebrated in the
southern states of India, particularly in Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh,
and Telangana. It marks the beginning of the Hindu lunisolar calendar
and signifies the onset of spring. Traditionally, Ugadi involves
cleaning homes, decorating with mango leaves, and creating intricate
rangoli designs. Special dishes like "Ugadi Pachadi," a mixture of
six tastes symbolizing different emotions, are prepared. The festival
is a time for family gatherings, prayers, and cultural performances
to welcome the new year with joy and positivity.
