Diwali

Diwali, also known as the Festival of Lights, is one of the most
significant Hindu festivals celebrated in India and around the world.
It symbolizes the victory of light over darkness and good over evil,
commemorating the return of Lord Rama to Ayodhya after defeating
Ravana. People decorate their homes with oil lamps, rangoli, and
colorful lights, and enjoy festive meals and sweets with family
and friends. Fireworks light up the sky, and prayers are
offered to Goddess Lakshmi for prosperity and well-being.
Diwali fosters a sense of community, joy, and renewal.
