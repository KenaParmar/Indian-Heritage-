Navratri

Navratri is a vibrant and widely celebrated Hindu festival
dedicated to the worship of Goddess Durga and her nine forms.
Spanning nine nights and ten days, it signifies the triumph of
good over evil, particularly the victory of Goddess Durga over
the demon Mahishasura. Each day is associated with a different
form of the goddess, and devotees engage in fasting, prayer, and
rituals. The festival is marked by lively cultural events, including
traditional dances like Garba and Dandiya, especially in the state
of Gujarat. Navratri culminates in Dussehra, which celebrates Lord
Rama's victory over Ravana, reinforcing the message of righteousness
and devotion.
